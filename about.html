All About Gaga:

Growing Up

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta "Lady Gaga" was born on March 28, 1986, in New York City to Cynthia Louise and Joseph Germanotta. She began playing the piano at the age of 4. From age 11, she attended the Convent of the Sacred Heart. Gaga described herself in school as, "... a misfit among her peers." The lessons taught her to create music by ear, which she preferred over reading sheet music. Her parents encouraged her to pursue music, and enrolled her in Creative Arts Camp.She also studied method acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.

The Story of Gaga

The Beginning of it All

From 2008-2010, Gaga let the world know who she was and made her mark with Breakthrough with The Fame and The Fame Monster. Then, from 2011-2014, she presented the world with: Born This Way, Artpop, and Cheek to Cheek. From 2015-2017, she participated in American Horror Story, released Joanne, and performed at the Super Bowl.
